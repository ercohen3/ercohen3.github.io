Why We Do It

Runway of Dreams empowers people with disabilities to have confidence and self-expression through fashion and beauty inclusion. We work to give people with disabilities access to fashion that excites and empowers them. We raise awareness, educate consumers, advocate for industry change, develop the next generation of design innovators and provide access to fashionable adaptive apparel.
